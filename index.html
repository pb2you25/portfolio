How It Works

From idea to upload

01

Brief

Tell me about your video. What's the concept, what moments matter, what's the vibe you're going for. The more detail upfront, the closer the first edit is to what you want.

02

Footage

Send me your VOD, stream recording, or raw clips. I'll work with whatever you have.

03

Edit

I put the video together based on your brief. Pacing, effects, sound, everything.

04

Review

You get the first version to review. Tell me what you'd tweak, I adjust.

05

Delivery

Final video, ready to upload.
